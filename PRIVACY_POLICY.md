# Privacy Policy for BanTayi

Effective date: May 4, 2026

BanTayi is an offline-first personal vault for tracking expiry dates, warranties, document dates, photos, and local reminders. This Privacy Policy explains how BanTayi handles information when you use the app.

This policy is provided for product and app store publication purposes. Replace the contact placeholder before publishing.

## Summary

BanTayi is designed so your vault data stays on your device. BanTayi does not require an account, does not use cloud sync, does not sell data, does not show ads, and does not use analytics or behavior tracking.

## Information You Add to the App

BanTayi lets you save information such as:

- Item names, categories, notes, storage locations, expiry dates, warranty dates, issue dates, and purchase dates.
- Photos or images you attach as visual proof, such as receipts, documents, IDs, or warranties.
- A local profile name or nickname used to personalize the app experience.
- A PIN setting used to lock access to the app.
- Local reminder preferences and scheduled local notification identifiers.

This information is stored locally on your device using on-device storage such as SQLite, SecureStore, and the app document directory.

## PIN and Security

BanTayi does not store your PIN in plain text. The app stores a salted SHA-256 digest for PIN verification. The PIN and related security settings are stored on your device.

## Photos, Camera, and Files

BanTayi may ask for camera or photo library access so you can attach images to vault items. Attached images are copied into the app's local document directory and are used only for the app features you choose to use.

BanTayi does not request microphone access.

## Notifications

BanTayi may ask for notification permission so it can schedule local reminders for expiry dates and warranties. These reminders are local scheduled notifications on your device. BanTayi does not use remote push notifications and does not use Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications.

## Optional Online Logo Search

BanTayi includes an optional online logo search feature. If you choose to search online for a logo, the search term may be sent to external services such as Wikipedia or Wikimedia Commons to find matching images. This feature is optional and only runs when you start an online logo search.

If you do not use online logo search, BanTayi is designed to keep vault data on your device.

## Data Collection and Sharing

BanTayi does not intentionally collect your vault data on our servers. BanTayi does not sell your data. BanTayi does not share your vault data with advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers.

Data may leave your device only in these limited situations:

- You choose to use the optional online logo search feature.
- You use normal operating system services, such as notification permission prompts, photo picker, camera, backups, or sharing features.
- You export, share, back up, restore, or otherwise move your own vault data.

## Backups and Restore

BanTayi may provide backup and restore tools. If you create a backup or share a backup file, you control where that file goes. Keep backup files private and store them securely.

## Children

BanTayi is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app is offline-first and does not require an account, BanTayi does not intentionally collect children's information on a server.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your vault data remains on your device until you delete items, remove app data, delete backup files you created, or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app may delete local app data depending on your operating system and backup settings.

## Third-Party Services

BanTayi may interact with:

- Your device operating system for camera, photo picker, secure storage, local notifications, and file storage.
- Wikipedia or Wikimedia Commons when you choose to use online logo search.

BanTayi does not integrate ads, analytics, Firebase, account systems, or remote sync services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The effective date at the top of this file will be updated when material changes are made.
